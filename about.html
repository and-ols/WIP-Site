About Me

Hola! I'm Aylin, a 25-year-old baker from Argentina with a lifelong love for everything sweet and savory that comes out of the oven. My passion for baking started young, watching my abuela whip up magic in the kitchen. The aroma of fresh bread and bubbling dulce de leche filling the house are some of my happiest memories.

Empanadas hold a special place in my heart. From the golden, flaky crust to the endless possibilities of fillings, they're the perfect embodiment of Argentinian comfort food. Through this website, I want to share my passion for baking, especially the art of empanadas, with all of you! So, come explore my recipes, discover new flavor combinations, and, most importantly, enjoy the delicious journey of baking together!
